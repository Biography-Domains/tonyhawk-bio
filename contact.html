Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Tony Hawk's official website?

No, this is a fan-run biography site. For official content, visit tonyhawk.com

How can I meet Tony Hawk?

Tony often appears at skateboarding events, video game launches, and charity events. Follow his social media for announcements.

Is Tony Hawk still skating?

Yes! While he retired from competition in 2003, Tony continues to skate regularly and shares videos on social media.
