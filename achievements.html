Historic Milestones

JUNE 27, 1999

First Documented 900

At the 1999 X Games in San Francisco, Tony Hawk became the first person to complete a 900 (two and a half rotations) in competition. This is widely regarded as one of the greatest moments in action sports history.

2021

Final 900 at Age 52

Landed his last 900 at age 52, proving his dedication to the sport. He announced this would be his final 900 due to physical limitations.
